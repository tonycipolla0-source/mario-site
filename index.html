Our Mission

MARIO, Inc. is a Kentucky-based nonprofit dedicated to promoting inclusion, respect, and community health through movement, wellness, and education.

Programs

  • Inclusive Fitness & Wellness
    Accessible classes and adaptive recreation.
  • Health & Nutrition Education
    Workshops for families and communities.
  • Community Engagement
    Events that reduce isolation and foster respect.
  • Leadership Development
    Empowering individuals of all abilities.
